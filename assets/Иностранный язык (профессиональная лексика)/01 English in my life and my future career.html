TASK 1

READING: ON LEARNING ENGLISH

Read the text «On Learning English». Make a list of new words and expressions.

When I think about my future career, I dream of becoming a highly qualified specialist. Nowadays, however, education plays a central role in the selection of men and women for jobs, which require high qualification. Moreover, it is impossible to make any career without good knowledge of foreign languages. I think English will be required regularly in my future work. I expect to use English in speaking and listening face to face and in telephone, radio, television communication. I also expect to use English in reading magazines professional journals, reports, newspapers, in reading and writing business letters. I will be using English with my colleagues and with members of community at large. I need English for broadly socio-cultural purposes: to obtain information, to convene at multinational gatherings, to travel. I know that nowadays at the airport or in a hotel bar a person of any nationality would address me directly in English without asking (me) whether1 speak the language: Some degree of command is taken for granted.

There are also some general interests and activities where I need English, such as: entertainment at home (radio, TV, etc.), friends, social gatherings (parties etc.), films, theatre, music, cultural interests, travel, sports, community activities, transportation, shopping, international exhibitions and many other needs.

TASK 4

Answer the following questions to discuss the details of the text.

1. What is your idea of your future career?

2. Is it possible to make any career without good knowledge of foreign language? Why?

3. In what professional and general activities will you need English in your future life and career?

4. How long have you been learning English?

5. Is learning English an easy thing?

6. How can you improve (perfect) your knowledge of English?

7. Have you ever learnt English outside the education system?

8. Is it expensive to learn English?

9. What other languages would you like to learn?

10. Why do the students of our College learn English?

11. Have you read any books (seen films) in English?

12. Have you ever been to England?
